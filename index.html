True March Madness-style bracket tree. Round of 32 is prefilled from active teams; future rounds populate from ESPN results, locked spreads, and takeover logic.

Pool Structure

  • 16 owners.
  • Each owner starts with 3 teams.
  • Owner score is credited to the current owner of a team/slot.

Group Stage Scoring

  • Win = 3 points.
  • Draw = 1 point.
  • Loss = 0 points.

Knockout Rules

  • Goal line that makes teams as even as possible is chosen when applicable.
  • DraftKings determines proper lines before each round.
  • Once lines are set, they do not change regardless of line movement.
  • If your team loses but covers the spread, you take over the winning team in the next round.

Prize Structure

  • Winner: $1,120
  • Runner-up: $480
