Takeover Rules

  • Spreads are graded at full time: 90 minutes plus stoppage time.
  • Extra time and penalties decide who advances, but do not change the spread result.
  • If a favorite advances but does not cover, the underdog owner takes over the advancing favorite.
  • If an underdog advances, that underdog owner keeps the team.
  • PK means no spread takeover. The advancing team keeps its current owner.
