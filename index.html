Pool Structure

  • Each owner starts with 3 teams.
  • Owner score follows the current owner of the advancing slot.

Group Stage Scoring

  • Win = 3 points.
  • Draw = 1 point.
  • Loss = 0 points.

Knockout Spread Rule

  • DraftKings/ESPN lines are locked before each round.
  • All lines are for regulation time only.
  • If a favorite advances in extra time or penalties, the underdog owner takes over the advancing team.
  • If the favorite wins in regulation but does not cover, the underdog owner takes over the advancing team.
  • Once lines are locked, they do not change.
